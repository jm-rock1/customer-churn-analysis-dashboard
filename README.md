# Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project analyzes customer churn data for a telecom company operating in California. The goal is to identify patterns among customers who churn, stay, or recently joined and determine potential drivers of customer churn.

## Business Questions
- What is the profile of a churned customer?
- How do churned, joined, and retained customers differ?
- What factors seem to drive customer churn?
- Is the company losing high-value customers?

## Tools Used
- Tableau
- Data visualization
- Calculated fields
- Row-level calculations
- Interactive filters and highlights

## Dashboard Features
- 4 visualizations exploring churn patterns
- Interactive filters to explore customer segments
- Highlight actions to emphasize churned customers
- Calculated fields to analyze customer value

## Key Insights
- Customers on month-to-month contracts showed higher churn rates.
- Certain service combinations were associated with increased churn risk.
- Some high-value customers were among churned segments.
